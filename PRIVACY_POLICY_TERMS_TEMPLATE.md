## Part 4: Key Differences from Other Audiobook Apps

Based on analysis of similar apps (Audible, Libby, Apple Books), Narrafied's policies should emphasize:

### Unique to Narrafied

1. **User-Uploaded Content**: Unlike traditional audiobook apps, users upload their own content
   - Clear copyright responsibility disclaimers
   - DMCA compliance procedures
   - Content restrictions

2. **Text-to-Speech Processing**: Content is converted, not pre-recorded
   - No guarantees on audio quality
   - Processing time disclaimers
   - Third-party TTS service usage

3. **Hybrid Payment**: Both Stripe and Apple IAP
   - Clear explanation of both options
   - Different refund procedures by payment method

### Similar to Other Apps

1. **Subscription Model**: Standard monthly subscription
2. **Progress Syncing**: Cross-device playback tracking
3. **Cloud Storage**: Server-based content storage
4. **Account Management**: Standard account features
